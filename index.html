Best Places to go in the Philippines


Philippines is a archipelago consist of 7, 1000 islands. It is also called as the Asia's pearl because of it's richness and the beauty of it's culture and landscape.


Manila is the Capital City of the Philippines. It is also known as the "Pearl of the Orient".
Tagaytay is one of the most visited places in the Philippines because of it's outstanding scenery. Baguio it is known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines. This is where you can explore whenever you are on summer vacation.
